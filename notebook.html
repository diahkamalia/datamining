Mendeskripsikan kemiringan/skewned dari data setiap variabel (bertipe numerik)

##Rumus Skewned $\( \operatorname{Kurt}[X]=\mathrm{E}\left[\left(\frac{X-\mu}{\sigma}\right)^4\right]=\frac{\mathrm{E}\left[(X-\mu)^4\right]}{\left(\mathrm{E}\left[(X-\mu)^2\right]\right)^2}=\frac{\mu_4}{\sigma^4}\)$

sns.distplot(c['xmin'],
             kde=True,
             bins= int(100/5),
             color='#E6B36F',
             hist_kws={'edgecolor':'black'},
             kde_kws={'linewidth':2}
             )
plt.title("Data Xmin")
